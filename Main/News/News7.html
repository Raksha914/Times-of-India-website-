Government has "high risk" warning for these Android users

The Indian Computer Emergency Response Team (CERT-In) has shared a warning for Android users. The cyber security agency, under the aegis of the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, said that it has discovered multiple vulnerabilities in Android software which can be exploited by an attacker to target users. CERT-In warning for Android users come with ‘High’ severity rating.

What CERT-In advisory reads

In its advisory, CERT-In says “Multiple vulnerabilities have been reported in Android which could be exploited by an attacker to execute arbitrary code on the targeted system.” It further adds: “These vulnerabilities exist in Android due to flaws in the Framework, System, Google Play system updates (ART and Wi-Fi subcomponent), Imagination Technologies components, MediaTek components, Qualcomm components and Qualcomm closed-source components.” Expand article logo Continue
